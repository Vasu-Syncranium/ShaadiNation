## Accessing the Admin Panel

1. Open your browser and go to: `admin.yourdomain.com`
2. Enter your admin token (provided separately)
3. Click **Login**

> **Important**: Keep your admin token private. Anyone with this token can modify your gallery.

---

## Uploading Photos

1. After logging in, you'll see the **Upload Images** tab
2. Select a category from the dropdown:
   - **Ceremony** - Wedding ceremony photos
   - **Reception** - Reception party photos
   - **Mehendi** - Mehendi function photos
   - **Sangeet** - Sangeet night photos
   - **Pre-wedding** - Pre-wedding shoot photos
3. Drag and drop your photos onto the upload area, OR click to browse
4. Wait for upload to complete (you'll see ‚úÖ for each successful upload)
5. Photos appear on the website **immediately**!

### Photo Tips
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP work best
- **Size**: Max 10MB per photo
- **Resolution**: Higher resolution = better quality, larger files

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can guests access the admin panel?
No. The admin panel is only available at `admin.yourdomain.com` and requires your private token.

### How quickly do photo changes appear?
Instantly! Upload or delete takes effect immediately.

### Is there a limit on photos?
The free plan allows ~10GB of storage, which is roughly 2,000-5,000 high-quality photos.

### What if I forget my admin token?
Contact your technical support person to reset it.

### Can I change the website text?
Text changes require code modifications. Contact your developer.
